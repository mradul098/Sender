This is a web application developed to create a news portal. Here, users can read news of their favorite category. Admin is the user who will add news to the application using add news feature. Also, it can view the category list of news which are most viewed. and the news is going to fetched by the help of News api. The project is created on MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS). The main purpose of the project is to provide hindrance less and fast access to daily news without baffling ads and useless information which sometimes divert the whole purpose of reading news.
